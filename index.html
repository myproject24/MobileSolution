About Ashwini Electronics

Smartphones drive pretty much every aspect of our lives today. From being alarm clocks, to diaries and notepads, cameras, communication means, and even virtual hangout places, it is impossible to imagine a day without these best buddies. And when they fail to function, no wonder our lives go into a turmoil. Finding a fast, reliable and cost-effective solution for repair of these gadgets is a problem that plagues many and that precisely, is what we love to help you with.

Ashwini Electronics started in 2006 with the idea of giving consumers unmatched quality repair services and reliable technical support for Smart Phone. The aim is to focus on becoming a one-stop shop for smart phone services which is 'convenient', 'agile', 'reliable' and 'pocket friendly' to the end user. Our team works tirelessly to get your smart phone back in shape. Our customer requirements are keys to us & we are determined to provide the Best in Class –Optimized Quality Solutions, Performance Results & Operational Excellence to provide Profitable Solutions to customers who come to us. We are committed to delivering top notch customer services and strive continually to be the most preferred destination for smartphone repairs through our services that are 'convenient', 'agile', 'reliable' and 'pocket friendly’.
